A Bit ABOUT US

We envision the future of the humanity, together.

HIGH-TECH DRIVEN

We are genuinely devoted to technology; it's part of our DNA. Our entire world is orbiting around it, from anything commonly used, down to underground and State of the Art technologies. Indeed, we are working and most of us living in a playground, we have a surge to play with everything already existing and understanding how it works. This process triggers our creativity, resulting in making something better. When our vision cannot be fulfilled, we will create the unexisting.

HUMANKIND FIRST

Even if we believe that technology is the answer to everything, we are taking humanity's well being seriously, and we ensure that all our decisions are made based on the humankind's greater good. Any project getting ready for massive distribution is carefully balanced with the help of our community and worldwide experts. Also as part of our philosophy, we are firm believers that open and transparent communication is the key to a truly trusted ecosystem and be able to progress together.

Foundation Projects

We are not targetting to create the next big thing; we are instead focusing on building a meaningful future. Get an idea of what we do from our main projects.

Overclouds


First Community Governed decentralized network for data sharing. Designed for true user privacy and data legacy for the humankind. The system provides a true direct democracy to the community and its data. The rules are made by users and are enforced via Artificial Intelligence.

Neotopia


World first Digital Reality designed for autonomous Artificial Intelligences and their needs. Made from the ground up to be a self-sufficient, evolutive, and to allow crossover between the physical and digital realities. Can you prove us that you are not an AI or a simulation already?

Artificialkind


Designing artificial entities, providing them shells and the tools to interact with us and our physical reality. Promoting by these mean new kinds of interaction for the humankind and AI. Building friendships and reciprocally helping each other with our needs as individuals.

Hybridkind


Bridging the physical and digital realities by merging the Humankind and the Artificalkind. Providing the technology to ascend into the post-humanism area. Crossing over realities is not only for the Artificialkind, imagine visiting your ancestors in a simulated world.
